Formal Epistemology Workshop 2018

FEW 2018

Toronto, June 12–14

This year's keynote speakers are Lara Buchak and Mike Titelbaum. The deadline for submissions is February 12. Submission is via EasyChair. Authors will be notified by May 31. Read the Call for Papers for more.

  • Call for Papers

Submit a Paper

What

Papers in formal epistemology, broadly construed. Papers should be in PDF format, be no more than 6,000 words, and include a 300 word abstract.

Where

Submissions should be made through EasyChair here.

When

The deadline for submissions is February 12. Authors will be notified of decisions by May 31.

How

Some travel funds are available for speakers. Amounts are still TBD, but we hope to fully reimburse all student and early-career speakers.

Keynotes

Lara Buchak

Lara Buchak is Associate Professor of Philosophy at the University of California, Berkeley. She is the author of Risk & Rationality and numerous papers in epistemology, decision theory, the philosophy of religion, and more.

Mike Titelbaum

Mike Titelbaum is Associate Professor of Philosophy at the University of Wisconsin—Madison. He is the author of Quitting Certainties and numerous papers in epistemology and ethics.
