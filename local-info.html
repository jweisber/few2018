Formal Epistemology Workshop 2018

Local Information

Location

All talks will be in the Jackman Humanities Building (JHB), the building that houses the University of Toronto’s philosophy department. JHB is located downtown at 170 St. George St. (opposite the St. George subway station if you're arriving by transit).

Airport

There are two airports that serve Toronto. Pearson International Airport (YYZ) is Toronto’s major airport. From Pearson, it takes approximately 40 minutes via cab to get to downtown Toronto ($60-$70), or one hour on public transportation ($6).

Toronto’s smaller airport is Billy Bishop Toronto City Airport (YTZ). Billy Bishop is located on the Toronto Islands, in downtown Toronto. From Billy Bishop, it takes under 15 minutes to get to most locations within downtown Toronto via cab, or 30 minutes on public transportation.

Accommodation

There are many options for accommodation within the vicinity of JHB. Here are a few suggestions:

  • Spadina 11 Guesthouse , located at 11 Spadina Road, is a six minute walk from JHB ($97/night).
  • Hotel 89 Toronto , located at 89 Avenue Road, is an 11 minute walk from JHB ($152/night).
  • The Downtown Home Inn Bed and Breakfast , located at 2 Monteith St., is a 25 minute walk from JHB ($129/night).
